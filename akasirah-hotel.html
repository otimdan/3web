Our Story

A Residence Above the City. A World Unto Itself.

Perched on Kololo Hill with sweeping views across Kampala, Akasirah was designed as a deliberate antidote to the ordinary. Our 64 rooms and suites blend contemporary East African craftsmanship with the warmth of Ugandan hospitality — handwoven textiles, carved ebony details, and gardens that breathe.

We are independently owned, locally staffed, and deeply rooted in the community that surrounds us. Every stay contributes to conservation programmes and artisan livelihoods across Uganda.

Culinary Experiences

Three Tables. One Philosophy.

Every dish at Akasirah begins at source — with farmers in Masaka, fishermen at Lake Victoria, and spice growers in the foothills. Our chefs transform what Uganda grows into cuisine that is contemporary, proud, and deeply flavourful.

  • The Kololo Terrace — Open-air rooftop dining with panoramic city views. International menu with Ugandan signature dishes. Breakfast through late dinner.
  • Enkya Restaurant — Our intimate fine-dining room. A curated tasting menu that changes with the harvest. Dinner only, reservations required.
  • The Garden Bar — All-day cocktails, artisan coffee, and light bites beneath the jacaranda canopy. The hotel's most beloved gathering spot.
